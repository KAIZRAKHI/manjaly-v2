About Us

Manjaly Infrastructure, founded by Mr. Jaison J Manjaly in 1994, is a leading construction company in South India, known for its commitment to quality and innovation in civil engineering.

Headquartered in Trichur, Kerala, we employ 125 professionals and have grown significantly over the past 30 years, delivering durable and precise construction solutions across the region.

With a mission to set new industry standards, our skilled team ensures excellence in every project, exceeding client expectations with reliability and superior craftsmanship.
